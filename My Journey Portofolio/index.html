My Study

Elementary School

SDN PAKIS III/370 Surabaya • 2006-2012

SDN Pakis III / 370 Surabaya is a public elementary school that is accredited A, this school was established on December 31, 1969. The location of SDN Pakis III / 370 is located on Jl. Dukuh Kupang Timur XII / 36 Kec. Sawahan, Surabaya. This school carries the concept of green, so that almost all areas in the school are covered with grass and various trees. Parking facilities are also extensive, which are available for car and motorcycle parking. Around the basketball court there is also a wide expanse of floor surrounding the field. The combination of the basketball court and this floor is very broad so it is usually used as a place for flag ceremonies.

Junior High School

SMP HANGTUAH 1 Surabaya • 2012-2015

Hang Tuah 1 Surabaya Middle School is a private school that is accredited A and the flagship school in the Surabaya branch of Hang Tuah Foundation. Hang Tuah 1 Middle School was established in 1958. The location of Surabaya's Hang Tuah 1 Middle School is on Jl. Bogowonto No. 57 Darmo Kec. Wonokromo, Surabaya. This school is unique in terms of color, which is dominantly dark blue, because this school is under the auspices of the navy. So even though the private school but the facilities do not disappoint and often get guidance from the TNI to always be disciplined.

Senior High School

SMAN 9 Surabaya • 2015-2018

Senior High School 9 Surabaya is one of the favorite State High Schools and includes a complex high school together with Surabaya Public High School 1, Surabaya Public High School 2, and Surabaya Public High School 5 in the City of Surabaya, East Java Province, Indonesia. Similar to high school in general in Indonesia, the school education period at Senior High School 9 Surabaya takes three years, from Class X to Class XII. SMA Negeri 9 Surabaya has a uniqueness that is very high solidarity from students to support the SMA Negeri 9 Surabaya team to compete (SONGOMANIA), so that other schools imitate what the Senior High School 9 Surabaya students do.

My State Campus

Universitas Negeri Surabaya • 2018-now

Surabaya State University (Unesa) is a state university in Surabaya, Indonesia, which was established on December 19, 1964. At present Unesa has 7 faculties; 4 faculties are in the Ketintang complex: the Faculty of Engineering, the Faculty of Economics, the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences, and the Faculty of Social Sciences. While 3 faculties are in the Lidah Wetan (LiWet) complex: the Faculty of Sports Science, the Faculty of Education as well as the Language and Arts Faculty. The current Chancellor of Unesa is Prof. Dr. Nurhasan, M.Kes. Meanwhile, the motto that guides this tertiary institution is "Growing with Character".

ORGANIZATION

OSIS (Intra School Student Organization)

In Senior High School 9 Surabaya • 2016-2018

OSIS (Intra School Student Organization) is the only student organization in the school environment. The purpose of the establishment of OSIS is to train students in organizing well and carrying out school activities related to students. Intra-School Student Organization is the only official student organization in the school and as a forum for student activities in schools with other coaching channels to support the achievement of the purpose of student coaching. Besides that OSIS as a motivator has a role to explore students' interests and talents and develop them through student council and extracurricular activities.

Information Technology Engineering Student Association (HIMTI)

In My Campus • 2018-2020

HIMTI is a student organization at the department level at Surabaya State University. This association is a forum for its members to develop mindsets and personalities related to their scientific discipline to be ready to enter the community. This association has members who support the implementation of the HIMA work program, and also have several departments. Here I was once a staff in the Interest and Talent Department, and for the current period I ask staff in the Department of Religion.
